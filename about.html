About Us

Coinbase, founded in 2012, is a global leader in cryptocurrency exchange, dedicated to creating an open and accessible financial system. Serving millions of users in over 100 countries, Coinbase provides a secure and user-friendly platform to buy, sell, and manage digital assets like Bitcoin and Ethereum. With a strong focus on trust, regulatory compliance, and cutting-edge technology, Coinbase empowers individuals and institutions to participate in the evolving world of decentralized finance.
